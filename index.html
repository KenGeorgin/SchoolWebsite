OUR HISTORY

Georgian Academy English Medium High School belonging to Kochi Diocese of Jacobite Syrian Christian Church . The school was established in 1983 and it is managed as Pvt. Unaided.There are 1,600 students(from LKG to 10th) and 80 staff are in the school.

The school emphasises a value based education, we give equal importance to values and academics. This institution is a leading institution in Ernakulam district in the area of academics and extracurricular activities. We secure 100% result in the SSLC and we are happy to say that this institution like a family to all who are associated with it. Georgian Academy English Medium High School was founded by Late Lamented Mor Osthatheos Thomas Metropolitan who was an illustrious teacher and educationist.
