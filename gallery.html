Code of Conduct


  • All pupils should attend the school morning
    assembly at 9.10 a.m.

  • Pupils should go in line to the school assembly
    and return to their classes
    in the same manner.

  • Pupils shall keep silence in the classes
    during the absence of teachers and
    in between two periods.
    Practice to speak in low voice.

  • Running,playing,or shouting inside the
    school building is not allowed.

  • Disrespect to any member of the
    staff in any form shall be considered
    as serious breach of discipline.

  • Pupils should attend the school
    in the prescribed uniform.

  • Pupils suffering from contagious
    or infectious diseases shall
    not be permitted

  • Pupils shall not be allowed to see
    their parents, guardians or
    visitors during the class hours,
    without permission.

  • Pupils shall strive to keep up the
    morale of the school by
    excellent performance in studies
    and pleasing good manners.

  • Borrowing money and other items
    is strictly forbidden
    in the school.

  • Leave letters should be brought on time.

  • Pupils shall solely be responsible
    for the safe custody of their books
    and other belongings.

  • Pupils are not allowed to leave
    the school without permission.

  • Costly items must not be worn
    or brought to the school.

  • It is forbidden to write on the walls
    and damage school materiials.
    Such damage will be compensated
    with fine equivalent to the cost
    of the materials.

  • Pupils, staff and parents should
    be loyal to the school and
    respect each other.

  • Be polite, courteous and obedient to
    everyone especially to elders.

  • Take pride in keeping ourselves,
    the premises of the school and
    the class rooms clean and tidy.
    Correction and advice from elders should be accepted
    gratefully and cheerfully.

  • Cultivate good manners and have a
    pleasant disposition where you are - at home,
    in school or outside.

  • Members of the staff are expected to
    do their best to draw
    out the best potentialities in
    each child.

  • In the classroom and in the premises
    the students must speak in ENGLISH.



    • Motto of our school: Glory of God and well being of man
